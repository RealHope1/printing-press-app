Frequently Asked Questions

What is the turnaround time for orders?

+

Turnaround time varies by service and quantity. Standard orders typically take 3-5 business days, while rush orders can be completed within 24-48 hours.

Do you offer design services?

+

Yes, we offer professional design services for an additional fee. Our design team can help create custom designs based on your requirements.

What file formats do you accept?

+

We accept high-resolution formats including PDF, AI, EPS, PSD, JPG, and PNG. Files should be at least 300 DPI for best print quality.

Do you deliver outside of Accra?

+

Yes, we offer delivery services throughout Ghana. Delivery fees vary based on location and order size.
