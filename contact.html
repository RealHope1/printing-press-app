Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical turnaround time?

+

Standard orders typically take 3-5 business days. Rush orders can be completed within 24-48 hours for an additional fee. Large volume orders may require additional time.

Do you offer design services?

+

Yes! We have a team of professional designers who can help create your designs from scratch or improve existing ones. Design services are available for an additional fee.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept cash, bank transfers, mobile money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), and major credit/debit cards. Payment terms can be discussed for bulk orders.

Do you deliver outside Accra?

+

Yes, we deliver throughout Ghana. Delivery fees vary based on location and order size. We also partner with courier services for nationwide delivery.

Can I see a sample before bulk printing?

+

Absolutely! We provide sample prints for approval before proceeding with bulk orders. This ensures you're completely satisfied with the quality and design.
